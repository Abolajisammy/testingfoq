Fountain of Qi is a Journey to Wellness

Wellness is the act of practicing healthy habits on a daily basis to attain better physical and mental well being, so you can thrive not just survive. At Fountain of Qi (pronounced chee) our mission is to introduce practices that are fun and accessible that nurture your mind, body and spirit.

Fountain of Qi literally means “Source of Energy.'' We will tap into the energy sources that help you Feel Good Create Vitality, Live Pain Free, Navigate emotional Turbulence, Calm the Nervous System & Become Aware of What Drives You and What Lights You Up.

This isn't some mythical spring; it's within you.We'll be your guide, your cheerleader, your fellow traveler on this extraordinary path.
Are you ready to step into your power?
Qigong

Helps reduce stress and anxiety, boosts energy, improves sleep, strengthens immunity, enhances balance and coordination, promotes emotional well-being, and supports healthy aging. Accessible to all ages and fitness levels—even from a chair—it offers a gentle yet powerful way to improve overall health and vitality.
Meditaion

Simple yet powerful practice that, with even a few minutes a day, can reduce stress and anxiety, improve focus, boost mood, increase self-awareness, enhance empathy, and support better sleep. There’s no single “right” way—what matters most is patience and finding the technique that works best for you.
Breathwork

Deepens mindfulness by anchoring you in the present, reduces stress and anxiety through calming the nervous system, improves emotional regulation by fostering thoughtful responses, and supports mental clarity and focus by boosting oxygen flow to the brain.
Massage

Helps reduce stress by lowering cortisol levels, boosts mood by increasing serotonin and dopamine, eases muscle tension and chronic pain, stimulates healthy circulation for better recovery, and enhances flexibility and range of motion by loosening joints and connective tissues.
